Pedro Gonçalves Oliveira (1986)

Pedro Gonçalves Oliveira is a musician from the north of Portugal born and raised in Guimarães who has collaborated and recorded with multifaceted projects and different music styles .

As a drummer and percussionist he has been involved in instrumental music through various musical styles ranging from Jazz to Rock, from traditional Portuguese sounds to improvised music for films and theater productions.

Degree in Music, variant of Electronic Music and Music Production. He works both as a live and studio session musician and has a studio specializing in drumming and percussion recording in his own Studio "PANCADA".
